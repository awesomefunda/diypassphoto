What we check

Every requirement, measured — with the actual numbers.

Head size & position

Crown-to-chin height as a share of the frame, eye line and centering — the geometry that fails most applications.

Background

How uniform it is and how far its colour drifts from target. A white wall often photographs grey — we catch it.

Pose & expression

Facing forward, head level, eyes open, neutral mouth. Profiles and big smiles get bounced.

Lighting & shadows

Even exposure and left-vs-right balance, so shadows under the nose or on the backdrop don't sink you.

Sharpness

A blur score — soft photos are a common rejection no checklist warns you about.

File specs

Exact pixels, aspect, JPEG and the file-size ceiling for digital uploads.

Glasses, head coverings and “is this really you” are human calls — DIYPassPhoto flags them to confirm rather than faking a score.
