**Long description (~150 words):**
DIYPassPhoto is a free, privacy-first passport and visa photo checker that runs entirely in your browser. Point your phone camera and the app measures your photo in real time — crown-to-chin head size, eye height, background colour (ΔE from the official target), lighting balance and sharpness — coaching you with on-screen fixes until every compliance gate passes. Capture, and it crops to the exact pixel dimensions and compresses under the file-size cap (e.g. ≤240 KB for the US DV Lottery), then hands you a download plus a compliance report.

It covers passport *and* visa specs for the US, UK, Canada, Australia, India, China, Japan, Germany and the Schengen area — including the tricky cases where visa rules differ from passport rules. Crucially, it measures without ever editing your photo, so your image stays compliant with the 2024-2026 US State Department rule that rejects AI-altered passport photos.
