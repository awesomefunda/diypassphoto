Australia Visa photo requirements

35 mm wide × 45 mm tall, plain light background, face 32–36 mm. Same biometric spec as the Australian passport. Verified from the Department of Home Affairs (homeaffairs.gov.au).

  • 35 mm wide × 45 mm tall, JPEG, under 1 MB for digital submission
  • Face 32–36 mm from chin to crown
  • Plain white or light background, no shadows
  • Neutral expression, eyes open, mouth closed
  • Even lighting
  • Taken within the last 6 months

Common rejection reasons

  • Coloured or patterned background
  • Face outside 32–36 mm range
  • Shadows on the face or background
  • Glasses or head coverings

Frequently asked questions

Is the Australian visa photo the same as the passport photo?

Yes — the Department of Home Affairs uses the same 35×45 mm biometric photo spec for both passports and visa applications.

What file size is allowed for an Australian visa photo?

Under 1 MB for digital online submission, in JPEG format.
