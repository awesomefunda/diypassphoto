Japan Passport photo requirements

35 mm wide × 45 mm tall, plain white or light background, face 32–36 mm. Verified from Japan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA — mofa.go.jp).

  • 35 mm wide × 45 mm tall, colour, JPEG
  • Face height 32–36 mm from chin to crown (about 34 mm ideal)
  • Plain white or light-coloured background, no shadows
  • Neutral expression, eyes open, looking straight ahead
  • No sunglasses; plain prescription glasses only if medically necessary
  • Taken within the last 6 months
  • No head coverings (except for religious reasons)

Common rejection reasons

  • Coloured or patterned background
  • Face too small or too large in the frame
  • Shadows behind the head or on the face
  • Photo taken more than 6 months ago
  • Smiling, open mouth, or head tilted

Frequently asked questions

What size is a Japanese passport photo?

35 mm wide × 45 mm tall, with the face (chin to crown) approximately 32–36 mm.

What background is required for a Japanese passport photo?

A plain white or light-coloured background. The background must be uniform with no shadows or patterns.

Can I wear glasses in my Japanese passport photo?

Sunglasses are not allowed. Plain prescription glasses are permitted only if removal is medically necessary.
