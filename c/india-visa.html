Official India photo requirements ↗

India e-Visa photo requirements

51×51 mm (2×2 inches), pure white background, face 70–80% of the frame, under 1 MB JPEG. The India e-Visa portal has stricter face-coverage rules than the passport. Verified from Indian Visa Online (indianvisaonline.gov.in).

  • 51×51 mm (square), JPEG, under 1 MB
  • Face covering 70–80% of the photo — stricter than the passport spec
  • Plain white background only
  • Neutral expression, eyes open, facing forward
  • No glasses, no head coverings (except religious if face fully visible)
  • Taken within the last 6 months

Common rejection reasons

  • Background not pure white
  • Face coverage outside 70–80% — most common rejection
  • File over 1 MB
  • Glasses or head covering
  • Shadows or red-eye

Frequently asked questions

What size is an India e-Visa photo?

51×51 mm square (same as passport), JPEG, under 1 MB. The face must cover 70–80% of the photo.

Why is my India e-Visa photo rejected?

The most common reason is that the face does not fill 70–80% of the photo. The background must also be plain white — not cream or grey.

Are glasses allowed in an India e-Visa photo?

No — the India e-Visa portal explicitly requires no glasses.
