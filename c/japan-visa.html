Official Japan photo requirements ↗

Japan Visa photo requirements

35 mm wide × 45 mm tall, plain light background, face 32–36 mm. Japan visa and passport photos share the same specification. Verified from Japan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

  • 35 mm wide × 45 mm tall, colour, JPEG
  • Face height 32–36 mm from chin to crown
  • Plain white or light background, no shadows
  • Neutral expression, eyes open, looking straight ahead
  • No sunglasses or tinted glasses
  • Taken within the last 6 months

Common rejection reasons

  • Coloured or patterned background
  • Face too small or large
  • Shadows on the face or background
  • Photo older than 6 months

Frequently asked questions

Is the Japan visa photo the same as the passport photo?

Yes — the same 35×45 mm specification applies to both Japanese passport and visa applications.

What size is a Japan visa photo?

35 mm wide × 45 mm tall, with the face roughly 32–36 mm from chin to crown, plain white or light background.
