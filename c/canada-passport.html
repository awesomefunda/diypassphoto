Canada Passport photo requirements

50 mm wide × 70 mm tall, white or light background, face 31–36 mm chin to crown. Canada's tall portrait format is unique — DIYPassPhoto frames it correctly and checks the rest live. Verified from IRCC (canada.ca).

  • 50 mm wide × 70 mm tall, colour, JPEG
  • Face height 31–36 mm from chin to crown (44–51% of 70 mm)
  • White or light-coloured neutral background
  • Neutral expression, mouth closed, eyes open
  • Looking directly at the camera
  • Even lighting, no shadows, no red-eye
  • Taken within the last 6 months, unmanipulated

Common rejection reasons

  • Wrong format — Canada uses tall 50×70mm not the square 2×2 inch used elsewhere
  • Face height outside 31–36 mm
  • Coloured or busy background
  • Shadows on the face or background
  • Head covering (unless for religious reasons)

Frequently asked questions

What size is a Canadian passport photo?

50 mm wide by 70 mm tall. The face (chin to crown) must be 31–36 mm — a different format from the square US/Australian photo.

Is the Canadian passport photo square?

No — it is a tall 50×70 mm portrait rectangle. Square crops or 2×2 inch photos are not accepted.

What background does a Canadian passport photo need?

A plain white or light-coloured background. It must be uniform with no shadows, patterns, or objects.
