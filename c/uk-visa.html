Official United Kingdom photo requirements ↗

UK Visa photo requirements

35×45 mm, plain white background, head 29–34 mm. UK visa photos require a white background (unlike passport photos which use light grey). Verified from UK Visas & Immigration (gov.uk).

  • 35 mm wide × 45 mm tall; digital at least 600×750 px, under 6 MB
  • Head 29–34 mm from chin to crown
  • Plain white background (note: visas require white, not light grey)
  • Neutral expression, mouth closed, eyes open
  • No glasses, no head coverings (unless religious/medical)
  • No shadows on face or background
  • Taken within the last 6 months, colour, unedited

Common rejection reasons

  • Light grey background (white required for visa — opposite of passport)
  • Head outside the 29–34 mm range
  • Shadows or reflections
  • Glasses or anything obscuring the face
  • Photo altered by software

Frequently asked questions

Is the UK visa photo different from the passport photo?

Yes — the key difference is the background. UK visas require a plain white background, while UK passport photos require a light-grey or cream background.

What size is a UK visa photo?

35 mm wide × 45 mm tall, at least 600×750 pixels for digital submission, under 6 MB.

How big should my head be?

29–34 mm from chin to crown — the same as for a UK passport photo.
